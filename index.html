About Me

Who I am?

Silent Max is a Ukrainian self-taught Musician and Composer. He has been obsessed with playing percussion and ethnic musical instruments since a young age.

During his musical journey, he mastered the skills of playing different musical instruments and started writing electronic music.

His music is characterized by uniqueness and authenticity. His philosophy is to feel the music and its flow, and he is not afraid to mix different genres or create new ones.

As a composer, Silent Max creates electronic, ethnic, and experimental music with a mixture of genres and a deep atmosphere and senses. In his tracks, he tries to express his inner feelings, convey his ideas, and create something unique and distinctive.
